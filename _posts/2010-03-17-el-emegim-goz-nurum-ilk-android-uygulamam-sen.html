SendMap Lite

The free SendMap Lite app from PhonoClick makes location sharing lightning-fast and easy.

  • Make your Tweets and Facebook updates more meaningful with map links.
  • Let your friends and followers know where you are, where you have been, or where you are going.
  • Share locations other than your current position, and even geotag your Tweets without actually going there. Great for setting up gatherings in advance.

Just launch SendMap Lite on your mobile, and wait a few seconds to see your location on the map. Make adjustments if needed, simply by moving the map on screen with you finger. You can also share a location other than your current position.
